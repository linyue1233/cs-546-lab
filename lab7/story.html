How to get my intership qulification

At the end of my junior year last year, I planned to fill my senior year with an internship experience, so I began to revise my resume and continue to deliver it, because my school is relatively poor, so I only got a few companies. Based on the project knowledge written on my resume, I started to review and learn some interview skills online. These are all I have never experienced before, so it is very painful at the beginning of the learning stage. I have to remember a lot of new knowledge and keep reviewing every day.

In early July last year, I started my interview process. There was an interview every two days. During the first interview, my speech was not smooth. And in the continuous interview process, I compiled interview notes and summed up my experience. Later, I became more and more confident during the interview, and finally got offers from several companies. I chose a listed company. My job is to do Java. End development, processing documents.

In that company, the people in the group took good care of me and taught me to write standardized code and enterprise-level solutions. In a year, I became a lot, able to build projects by myself, and think of a solution to a problem.
